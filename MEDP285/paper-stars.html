How to make an Origami Star

Materials:

  • Origami Paper Strips
    • - You can either buy precut origami paper strips or cut your own from square origami paper. Strips should be of equal width and length.

  • Glue or Double-Sided Tape (Optional)
    • - If you want a more secure hold, you can use glue or double-sided tape at the end.

Steps:

  1. Fold the strip of Paper
    2. - Take one end of the strip and make a knot in the shape of a hexagon on one end.

  2. Crease and made the Knot of the Star
    3. - Fold the smaller of the two tails under the knot and your left with a hexagon on one end and a long tail on the other.

  3. Keep Wrapping the strip around itself
    4. - Wrap the tail of the strip around your hexagon until the tail is too small too wrap.

  4. Tuck and Inflate the Star
    5. - Fold the remaining tail under the last wrap you finished. Then inflate the hexagon my pinching each corner.
